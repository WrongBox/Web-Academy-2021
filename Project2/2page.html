Hope and Despair

Hope is a very useful thing that helps humans get motivated and stay on track. We need motivation to get humans through the day and to succeed in life.

Hope

Hope can be in everyone, hope helps us to be inspired and creative. Hope can be shown as anything , a object, a person , a speech , or anything. Others rely on hope because they don't want to face reality , the reality is that this world is full of despair.
